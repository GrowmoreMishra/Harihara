About Us

Hari Hara Textiles is a premier provider of high-quality textiles

specializing in ladies' apparel, men's clothing, and an elegant range of scarves and stoles. We are committed to delivering superior products that meet the highest standards of craftsmanship, with a focus on innovative digital printing technology and exceptional customer service.

Our advanced in-house capabilities, including state-of-the-art digital printing machines, enable us to offer unique, customizable textiles for designers, boutique owners, and brand owners. We pride ourselves on our ability to accommodate low minimum order quantities (MOQ), allowing for greater flexibility and accessibility to our esteemed clients. Furthermore, our sampling service ensures that your designs are brought to life exactly as envisioned before committing to a full production run.
